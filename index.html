The Dish Saftey Process

  1. CHEFF VETTING - We start by meeting with each potential Chef in their kitchen to view the masters at work. We review the cooking surfaces & utensils to ensure they are adequate for food preparation. Finally we taste test all dishes that will be ready to sell. We also perform random taste tests to ensure quality is maintained over time. 
  2. FOOD HANDLING CERTIFICATE - All of our cooks are sponsored to complete the Food Handling certificates needed to work with and prepare food in their respective Province or State. 
  3. CUSTOMER REVIEWS - Our cooks love to hear from their patrons! Whether its rave reviews or areas that could use improvement, our cooks value the voice of their customers.
