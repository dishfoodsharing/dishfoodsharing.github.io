Who We Are

Dish Food Sharing is on a mission to make higher quality, healthier food available for pickup & delivery in your neighbourhood.  
      
Whether you're trying to eat healthier and don't know where to start, don't have time to prepare a meal or just want to expand your culinary palette; Dish helps you eat better food, that's sourced by the people who know about it best. 

Dish believes that eating healthy shouldn't be difficult.

By connecting you with local suppliers with a passion for food, you'll be able to eat healthier, save time and money as well as build relationships in your community.
