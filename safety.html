The Dish Saftey Process

  1. VETTING - We start by meeting with each potential supplier to ensure they are abiding by all safety standards. Whether in their kitchen to view the masters at work or behind the scenes where food is stored. We also do random quality tests to ensure quality is maintained over time. 
  2. FOOD HANDLING CERTIFICATE - All of our cooks are required to complete the Food Handling certificates needed to work with and prepare food in their respective Province or State. All of our Grocery Suppliers are required to have food safety plans & adhere to all local grocer regulations.
  3. CUSTOMER REVIEWS - Our partners love to hear from their patrons! Whether its rave reviews or areas that could use improvement, they value the voice of their customers. We also use your reviews to ensure all partners are providing exeptional quality and take customer feedback seriously.
